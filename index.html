The Journey Ends

One of the most critically-acclaimed entries in the Star Wars saga will return for its epic conclusion with 12 all-new episodes on Disney+ beginning February 21. Today we got our first look at the colorful trailer for the final season of Star Wars: The Clone Wars, with glimpses from three exciting new story arcs, which you can watch below. From Dave Filoni, director and executive producer of The Mandalorian, the new Clone Wars episodes will continue storylines introduced in the original series exploring the events leading up to Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith.
